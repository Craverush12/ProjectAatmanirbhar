The seed library shelves are filling with labeled jars and handwritten notes. Each variety carries a story about drought, flavor, and medicine. This season the collective trained new youth archivists and exchanged seeds with neighboring hamlets. Next: documenting germination data and sharing an open ledger for the valley.
