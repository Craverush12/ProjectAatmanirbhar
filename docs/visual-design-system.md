# Visual Design System — Project Aatmanirbhar

Premium documentary + community movement aesthetic, rooted in Yamuna Valley.

## Color Palettes (3 modes)
- **River Dawn (default):** mist ivory `#f6f2ea`, river teal `#1c8c8a`, stone gray `#9aa4ae`, saffron accent `#ffb341`, ink `#0b0f14`.
- **Night River (dark):** deep ink `#050912`, teal highlight `#5fd3cf`, warm sand text `#e8efff`, saffron spark `#ffd27a`, slate `#4f5e79`.
- **Field Notes (alt):** paper `#f7f1e3`, charcoal `#1f1b16`, muted green `#6c8a6a`, clay `#c4a27a`, graphite lines `#5a4c3c`.

## Typography
- **Headlines:** DM Serif Display (or Fraunces fallback) — elegant, high contrast.
- **Body:** Inter (or Manrope fallback) — modern, calm.
- **Utility/Data (optional):** JetBrains Mono for labels/stats.
- Guidance: Tighten letter-spacing on display (-1%), comfortable line-height 1.45 for body, 1.2 for headlines.

## Spacing & Grid
- **Grid:** 12-col desktop, 6-col tablet, 4-col mobile; 80px gutters desktop / 32px mobile.
- **Whitespace rhythm:** XL sections 120–160px top/bottom; cards 24–32px padding; hero copy max width 60ch.
- **Containers:** Max width 1280px, with occasional full-bleed media strips.

## Components & Patterns
- **Hero:** Full-bleed photo/video, ink-to-teal gradient overlay, dual CTAs, stat chip.
- **Impact Strip:** Animated counters with mono labels, over blurred strip.
- **Pillar Cards (Vayu/Jal/Bhoomi):** Icon/mini-illustration + one-line promise + micro-tag.
- **Story Modules:** Photo essay grid; field diary card (location tag, 1–2 sentences).
- **Trust Modules:** Partner logos row, report downloads, transparency blurb.
- **CTA Banners:** Bold gradient on brand ink/teal, two CTAs.
- **Gallery:** Masonry/editorial grid with varying crops; hover reveal caption.
- **Timeline:** Horizontal scroll on mobile, stepped vertical on desktop.
- **Quote Blocks:** Large serif quote, small-caps source, subtle underline.
- **Map Module:** Yamuna Valley snapshot with pins and legend.

## Motion Principles
- Slow, calm: 200–400ms fade/translate (8–14px).
- Scroll reveals staggered by 60ms.
- Hover: lift + soft shadow; buttons scale 1.02, cards translateY(-4px).
- Avoid gimmicks/parallax; prefer micro camera-move overlays or gradient shimmer.

## Media Style Guide
- **Documentary photos:** Natural light, real people, faces + hands at work, slight grain.
- **Drone/Landscape:** River at dawn, ghats, farms, mist; warm saffron sun + teal water.
- **Macro textures:** Soil, water ripples, leaves, woven fabric, brass.
- **Icons:** Thin-line, nature + science cues (leaf/airflow/water drop/grid).
- **Color grading:** Warm shadows, cool highlights; keep skin tones natural.
